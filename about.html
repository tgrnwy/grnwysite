About

About

“Work that treats existence itself as a form of monumental resistance.”

T.E. Greenway is a UK-based artist working across printmaking, soundwork and installation. The practice interrogates the weight of presence: the way things persist, accumulate and endure.

A recurring language of monolithic forms, torn surfaces and luminous orbs runs through the work, from the sketchbook to the installation environment.
